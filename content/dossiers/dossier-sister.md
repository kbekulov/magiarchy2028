# Dossier - Sister

Sister is never casual about closeness.

Where others flirt with symbolic pressure, she inhabits it with conviction.
Her care can feel warm, but it is never loose. She notices fracture early and
often answers it with form, liturgy, and a gaze that expects coherence.

## Interpretive Note

- Chamber function: stabilizes the interior when it threatens to become pure improvisation.
- Dramatic value: devotion, correction, tenderness sharpened into demand.
- Bureau risk: her certainty can make secular procedure look embarrassingly temporary.
