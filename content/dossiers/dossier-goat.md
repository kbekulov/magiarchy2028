# Dossier - Goat

Goat is ridiculous only if you mistake ridiculousness for harmlessness.

He punctures ceremony at exactly the moment ceremony begins to believe in itself.
What sounds like appetite is often diagnosis. What sounds like mockery is often the
most accurate reading in the room.

## Interpretive Note

- Chamber function: prevents solemnity from becoming fraud.
- Dramatic value: absurdity, appetite, irreverence, dangerous truth-telling.
- Bureau risk: procedure hates him because he is often correct at indecent speed.
