# Dossier - Anima

Anima does not merely tempt; she clarifies by pressure.

Her presence changes the temperature of a room without changing its manners.
What she offers is rarely vulgar and almost never safe. She makes subtext visible,
then behaves as if it was obvious all along.

## Interpretive Note

- Chamber function: exposes what the polite version of a scene is trying to hide.
- Dramatic value: wit, charge, destabilization, and lucid cruelty when needed.
- Bureau risk: she makes professional distance feel theatrical rather than real.
