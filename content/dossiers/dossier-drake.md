# Dossier - Drake

Drake believes in consequences before explanations.

He is useful precisely because he understands that some rooms only become honest
when they realize there is an outside. His restraint is real, but so is the danger
of leaning on him too often.

## Bureau Read

- Public function: security, retrieval, scene control.
- Private burden: too good at acting like inevitability.
- Chamber effect: the interior refuses to be intimidated, which makes him think harder than he likes.
