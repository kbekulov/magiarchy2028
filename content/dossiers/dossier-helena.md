# Helena

Helena is currently defined most clearly by function. She builds the framing logic around Lynleit and converts internal shock into durable control.

## Operational Role

- Builds the accusation vector after Fionn's assassination.
- Redirects MSF machinery into pursuit.
- Consolidates authority under unstable conditions.

## Current Canon Status

Her full personal history remains open, but her structural use is already clear: she is the figure who proves that institutions can mutate into weapons while still speaking the language of order.

## Writer Note

She should read as precise and dangerous, not melodramatic. Her force comes from systems capture rather than open theatricality.
