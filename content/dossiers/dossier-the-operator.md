# Dossier - The Operator

The operator runs the bureau with the manner of someone who understands how much
damage calm can conceal.

He is not the loudest presence in the chamber, but many of its conflicts bend
toward him anyway. He carries procedure like a courtesy, not a shield.

## Bureau Read

- Public function: receives incidents, interviews witnesses, writes the first version of events.
- Private burden: keeps discovering that the first version is the one most likely to betray someone.
- Chamber effect: every serious case drags him inward, toward wit, tension, and symbolic exposure.
