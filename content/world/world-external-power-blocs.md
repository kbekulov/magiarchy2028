# External Power Blocs

## Aristocracy

Legacy, inheritance, noble influence, and succession logic continue shaping how authority is distributed.

## Government

Government represents the visible machinery of state legitimacy, parliamentary oversight, and public-order language.

## Church

The Church knows enough to fear both nightmare and the Magi who suppress it. It cooperates because it must, but suspicion is part of the structure rather than a temporary misunderstanding.
