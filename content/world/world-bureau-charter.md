# Bureau Charter - Why the Office Exists

The bureau was never meant to be grand.
It was meant to be precise.

Its authority comes from speed, discretion, and the ability to hold unstable material
without immediately translating it into government theater.

## Bureau Ethos

- Investigate first, narrate second.
- Protect witnesses without romanticizing them.
- Never assume the city wants the truth more than it wants continuity.
