# Ontological Foundation

Magic is not an external force imposed on the world. It is woven into reality itself through psyche, soul, emotion, and structural pressure.

## Consequences

- Human nightmare can externalize when the equilibrium of a city starts to fail.
- Such manifestations are never generic; each bears the imprint of the people feeding it.
- Natural law remains absolute, but not seamless. Reality contains exploitable cracks, seams, and pressure points.
