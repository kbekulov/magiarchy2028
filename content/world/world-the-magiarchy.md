# The Magiarchy

The word **Magi** names the broader class. The **Magiarchy** names the sanctioned order within it.

## Core Role

- Organizes Church-aligned Magi through regional Magiarchates.
- Negotiates externally while preserving the institutional terms of Magi survival.
- Makes magical power politically legible without making it public.

## Church Relations

- Productive, necessary, and never innocent.
- Built on diplomacy, shared necessity, espionage, extortion, and occasional blood.
- Trust is always conditional.
