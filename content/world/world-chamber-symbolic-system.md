# Chamber System - Symbolic Pressure

The chamber is not powered by desire alone, but desire is one of its most precise
instruments.

What matters is not explicitness. What matters is charge: who draws near, who holds
back, who speaks as if the room were listening, and who discovers that the room is.

## Design Rule

- Keep the atmosphere intimate without becoming graphic.
- Let wit and symbolic tension carry more force than disclosure.
- Treat the chamber as a living dramaturgy rather than a puzzle box.
