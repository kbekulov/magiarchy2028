# The Country

The national structure is organized around four pressure points.

- **Velna-Nue**: visible law and parliamentary legitimacy.
- **Feldenmark**: inherited sovereignty and aristocratic continuity.
- **Turon**: intelligence leverage and controlled opacity.
- **Naminsk**: movement control, trade, and economic access.

## Turon

Turon matters most directly to the current canon.

- Semi-autonomous and operationally insulated.
- Headquarters zone for Lynleit-family MSF operations.
- Feared because it is both indispensable and deniable.
