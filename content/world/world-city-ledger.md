# City Ledger - The District Arrangement

The city organizes knowledge by district before it organizes it by truth.

Administrative boundaries determine which incident can be ignored, which must be
contained, and which becomes a political offering to the Crown.

## Core Notes

- The Ninth District keeps the oldest sealed records.
- Mirrorgate handles theatre, rumor density, and cultural contagion.
- Government language often treats chamber-adjacent incidents as maintenance failures.
