# Law Of Magistry

Magic does not violate natural law. It manipulates it from within.

## Core Distinctions

- **Thaumaturgy** handles outward manipulation: ignition, reinforcement, weakening, and environmental effect.
- **Theurgy** handles inward transformation: perception, awareness, identity, and emotional-cognitive recalibration.
- No clean line fully separates inner and outer change.

## Limitations

- Magic cannot create absolute contradiction.
- It cannot create from nothing or overwrite foundational structure.
- Every intervention shifts equilibrium and risks backlash.

## Rule Of Power

The greatest Magi are defined by precision and restraint, not volume.
