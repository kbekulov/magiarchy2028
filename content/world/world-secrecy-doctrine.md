# Secrecy Doctrine And Governance

Knowledge is distributed as little as possible and only as widely as necessary.

## Governance

- A hidden magical society regulates practice to prevent destabilization.
- The Church knows enough to cooperate while remaining suspicious.
- Magi outside sanctioned order become hunted renegades rather than merely dissidents.

## Why Secrecy Holds

- Full exposure risks collapse, reprisals, internal conflict, and civil panic.
- The Magi suppress horrors partly to save civilization and partly to prove they should not be eradicated.
- Unchecked miracle-making is treated as civilizational risk, not individual eccentricity.
