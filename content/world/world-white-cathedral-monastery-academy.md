# White Cathedral-Monastery-Academy

The Academy is cathedral, monastery, and university at once.

## Spatial Logic

- Anchored to reality but not permanently fixed to one geography.
- Rarely relocates, and only when magical-political stress becomes serious.
- Recognized through selective ontological acknowledgement rather than simple illusion.

## Atmosphere

- White stone, vaulted scale, cold mornings, lingering echoes.
- Beautiful, severe, bureaucratically heavy.
- Built to study and contain loopholes in natural law.
