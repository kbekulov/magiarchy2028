# Hidden History And Balance Of Powers

Magic endured not through open conquest, but through restraint, containment, and deliberate silence.

## Hidden History

- The Magi divided between Church-aligned order and rejectionist independence.
- From that split emerged the Magiarchy and its regional Magiarchates.
- Modern society believes itself more secular and transparent than it really is.

## Balance Of Powers

- The Church, Magiarchy, aristocracy, government, and intelligence structures depend on each other while mistrusting each other.
- No bloc is fully sovereign over the hidden world.
- Betrayal inside this equilibrium can quickly become bloody.
