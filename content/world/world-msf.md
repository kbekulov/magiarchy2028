# MSF

MSF begins as a private intelligence structure and becomes the continuity core of a deeper magical transformation.

## Narrative Function

- Carries institutional memory across crisis.
- Provides the setting with an intelligence grammar as well as a magical one.
- Demonstrates that continuity can survive through doctrinal mutation instead of institutional death.

## Identity Doctrine

MSF keeps its name before and after transformation. The acronym is not revealed because function matters more than formal definition.
