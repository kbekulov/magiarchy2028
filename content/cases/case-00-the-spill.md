# Event File - The Spill

The Spill is the earliest active rupture in the current timeline scaffold. It is less a solved incident than a pressure pocket: a covert deployment, a contaminated perimeter, and the beginning of Heyk's usable value to actors outside his original lane.

## Known Function

- It gives the story its first operational texture.
- It begins Heyk's arc before the central MSF succession crisis becomes visible.
- It creates the context for Sherie and Drake to extract him and extend a new offer.

## Pressure Chain

- Hidden danger enters before institutional language is ready for it.
- Extraction becomes more important than explanation.
- The event establishes that peripheral operators may become central once the system destabilizes.
