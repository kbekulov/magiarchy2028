# Event File - MSF Compression

MSF survives collapse by changing shape instead of surrendering continuity. The institution becomes more secretive, more guild-like, and more dependent on selective trust.

## Structural Mutation

- The name remains even as doctrine changes.
- The acronym is intentionally unrevealed.
- Operators, facilitators, and non-central personnel still matter after compression.

## Why It Matters

- This event keeps the story from becoming a simple rise-and-fall narrative.
- Institutional survival becomes as important as personal survival.
- The crisis transforms MSF into a more explicitly magical architecture without erasing its intelligence roots.
