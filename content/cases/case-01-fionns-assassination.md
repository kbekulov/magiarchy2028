# Event File - Fionn's Assassination

This event is the story's decisive break. Helena weaponizes the murder politically, using accusation as both internal control and succession architecture.

## Trigger Effect

- Lynleit is forced out of stable authority.
- MSF resources are turned inward and repurposed for the hunt.
- Private crisis becomes institutionally legible.

## Counterweight

Kyrien becomes essential at the moment ordinary chains of command fail. His value is not formal rank, but contingency: extraction, unpredictability, and the ability to act where public authority cannot.

## Story Consequence

- The narrative moves from inheritance pressure into exile.
- Trust becomes expensive.
- Every later recovery attempt is shaped by this event.
