# Case 01 - The Amber Ward

The bureau is asked to treat the ward failure as an infrastructure dispute.
It is not an infrastructure dispute.

Witnesses describe heat without flame, phrases repeating across unrelated mouths,
and a sealed lower archive answering to names that should have remained ceremonial.

## Bureau Position

- Public explanation: containment review and historical survey.
- Private explanation: the chamber has begun selecting its witnesses.
- Political risk: if the Crown identifies the breach first, the bureau loses custody of the narrative.

## Initial Assignments

1. The operator enters below street level and confirms live response.
2. Sherie manages witness interviews and rumor discipline above ground.
3. Drake watches the district perimeter for any second incident disguised as coincidence.
