# Global Story Prompt

Use this file as the current canon pack for story generation, scene planning, and world-consistent writing.

## Story Universe

- Magic is not external to reality; it is woven into psyche, soul, and pressure points within natural law.
- When the inward life of a city destabilizes, nightmare may externalize into material threat.
- The Magi exist to detect, contain, and suppress those breaches before the public understands what it has seen.

## Hidden Political Order

- Stability depends on an uneasy balance between the Magiarchy, the Church, aristocracy, government, and intelligence structures.
- The central conflict is truth versus stability, not good versus evil.
- Exposure of the hidden architecture threatens both civilization and the institutions that currently contain it.

## Active Plot Scaffold

- Helena frames Lynleit for Fionn's assassination.
- Lynleit is forced into exile.
- Kyrien becomes the hidden counterweight.
- Tien serves Helena as the mirror threat.
- MSF mutates rather than disappearing, compressing into continuity architecture.

## Usage Rule

When writing new material, preserve precision, institutional pressure, and consequence. Magic must never feel cheap, infinite, or consequence-free.
