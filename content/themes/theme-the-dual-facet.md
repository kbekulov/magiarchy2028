# Theme Note - The Dual Facet

The bureau gives the project procedure, public risk, and case logic.
The chamber gives it symbolic pressure, wit, intimacy, and archetypal heat.

Neither side is complete without the other.
If the bureau becomes too dominant, the story dries into procedure.
If the chamber becomes too dominant, the story loses external consequence.
