# Theme Note - Relationship Dynamics

The cast should feel archetypal without becoming flat.

They are not fantasy roles in costume.
They are dramatic pressures with preferences, vanity, intelligence, and memory.

## Tone Rule

- Keep the wit sharp.
- Keep intimacy suggestive rather than explicit.
- Let symbolic conflict do more work than spectacle.
