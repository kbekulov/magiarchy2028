# Visible Authority And Shadow Capability

Lynleit and Kyrien are not built around romance-first tension. Their real engine is structural complementarity.

- She accepts visibility as a responsibility, but leads most naturally through pattern-reading and selectively granted proximity.
- Her style and bodily composure are not decoration; they are part of how she regulates threshold, attention, and distance.
- He protects through obscurity, contingency, and selective intervention.
- Their tension comes from whether private ethical perception can survive public authority while accepting support it cannot fully witness.
