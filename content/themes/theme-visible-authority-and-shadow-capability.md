# Visible Authority And Shadow Capability

Lynleit and Kyrien are not built around romance-first tension. Their real engine is structural complementarity.

- She leads through visibility, responsibility, and proximity.
- He protects through obscurity, contingency, and selective intervention.
- Their tension comes from whether ethical visible power can survive while accepting support it cannot fully witness.
