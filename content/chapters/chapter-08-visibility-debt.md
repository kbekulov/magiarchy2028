# Chapter 8 - Visibility Debt

Every hidden intervention creates a bill that visible authority eventually has to pay.

## Narrative Tension

- Lynleit must absorb outcomes she did not fully witness.
- Kyrien's shadow work becomes harder to keep morally abstract.
- Tien's pressure turns direct enough to demand confrontation.

## Writer Rule

Do not let secrecy read as convenience. It must always produce cost.
