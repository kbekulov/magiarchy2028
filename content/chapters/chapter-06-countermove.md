# Chapter 6 - Countermove

Countermove is the first chapter where recovery becomes active strategy instead of endurance.

## Pressure Movements

- Lynleit begins repositioning for visible return.
- Kyrien escalates from extraction to anti-pressure design.
- Wider governance actors start responding as the crisis becomes harder to contain privately.

## Story Logic

Recovery should feel expensive, partial, and real.
