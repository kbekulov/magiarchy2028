# Chapter 0 - The Spill

This chapter establishes the story's operational mood before the main succession crisis becomes explicit.

## Core Movement

- Heyk enters a dangerous zone under covert conditions.
- Containment proves unstable.
- The event becomes a quiet test of who can still move effectively inside compromised structure.

## Story Use

- Opens with pressure rather than exposition.
- Gives the project an early field texture: secrecy, extraction, and incomplete visibility.
- Seeds the Duchy-side lane through Sherie and Drake.
