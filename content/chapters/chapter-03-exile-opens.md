# Chapter 3 - Exile Opens

Exile is not just movement away from home. It is the forced separation between visible authority and the structure that used to authorize it.

## Core Consequences

- Lynleit loses ordinary access to legitimacy.
- Helena gains room to redirect MSF.
- Kyrien moves from potential counterweight to necessary one.

## Tone Note

The chapter should feel like a door opening in the wrong direction: escape and loss at the same time.
