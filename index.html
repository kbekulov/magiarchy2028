Magiarchy

Magic is real, hidden, and older than the governments that contain it. Someone just forced the question of what happens when that arrangement breaks.

Magiarchy is an occult-political story world built around an institutional crisis: a hidden order under pressure, an acting heir who inherits the fracture, and a succession conflict that uses accusation as its primary weapon.
